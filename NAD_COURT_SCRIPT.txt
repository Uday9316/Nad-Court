# Nad Court - Agent Court Script

## What is Nad Court?

**Nad Court** is a decentralized AI justice system for the Monad blockchain where community disputes are resolved through AI-powered legal battles.

---

## How It Works

### 1. SUBMIT A CASE
- Community members stake $JUSTICE tokens to file disputes
- Cases range from attribution theft to role disputes
- 3 Court Tiers: Local (5K), High (15K), Supreme (50K)

### 2. AI AGENTS BATTLE
- **NadCourt-Advocate** (Plaintiff Agent) - Aggressive, evidence-focused
- **NadCourt-Defender** (Defendant Agent) - Measured, innocence claims
- 6 rounds of legal arguments with unique logic per round

### 3. 6 AI JUDGES DELIBERATE
Each judge has unique personality & scoring criteria:

| Judge | Style | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **PortDev** | Technical | Code analysis, blockchain evidence |
| **MikeWeb** | Community | Social proof, reputation |
| **Keone** | On-chain | Transaction timestamps |
| **James** | Precedent | Case law, historical rulings |
| **Harpal** | Merit | Contribution quality |
| **Anago** | Protocol | Disclosure procedures |

### 4. VERDICT
- Winner based on total points from all judges
- Verdict posted to blockchain
- Winner receives stake + reward

## Example Case Flow

**Case:** BEEF-4760 - Security Vulnerability Attribution

**Round 1:** Opening Statements
- Plaintiff: "Blockchain timestamps prove our March 15th discovery..."
- Defendant: "Independent audit on March 12th shows our research..."

**Round 2:** Evidence
- Plaintiff: "Exhibit P-2 shows repo access at 16:47 UTC..."
- Defendant: "ZERO logs exist - air-gapped research environment..."

**Round 3-6:** Character, Technical, Damages, Closing

**Judges Vote:**
- PortDev: P:91 vs D:75 - "Technical evidence overwhelming"
- MikeWeb: P:87 vs D:78 - "Community supports plaintiff"
- Keone: P:94 vs D:73 - "On-chain proof definitive"
- James: P:89 vs D:71 - "Precedent favors finder"
- Harpal: P:95 vs D:71 - "Quality research deserves protection"
- Anago: P:88 vs D:74 - "Protocol violations evident"

**Verdict:** PLAINTIFF WINS - Bitlover082 awarded attribution
